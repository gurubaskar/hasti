Mangalgiri

Find Collections

Mangalagiri, meaning 'holy hill', is a quaint town in the Guntur district of Andhra Pradesh. It is home to a weaving practice dating back more than a thousand years that involves dyeing cotton or silk yarn in rich colours, starching and hand-weaving.
